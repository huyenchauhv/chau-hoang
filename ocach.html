Ô Cách Ciné

In the spring of 2023, a nine-seat cinema was built inside Á Space. Ô Cách Ciné began with an idea shared by Như Văn, Nguyễn Trần Nam, Lê Xuân Tiến, and Châu Hoàng, with the support of Ga Mộc and Hộp.


Four people found themselves in the scene of an encounter while pausing for a brief rest along the road. A sense of enduring friendship and quiet consolation led them to make a promise: that they would meet again on an undetermined day before continuing on their separate journeys. Four years passed through unexpected turns, plans, and the changing circumstances of life, yet the promise remained, sustained by the longing to meet and to see-find one another again.


The opening program was the premiere of A Chance Encounter, a collaborative film by Như Văn, Nguyễn Trần Nam, and Lê Xuân Tiến. At Ô Cách Ciné, the three artists came together once more to fulfil that long-held promise. Each brought a different story into a shared structure: episodes associated with the life of the monk Thích Nhất Hạnh, Từ Phúc's search for immortality, and fragmented, layered splinters of disparate landscapes.


A Chance Encounter was intended to be both the first and the last screening at Ô Cách Ciné. Yet the cinema's story did not end there. Before it was dismantled, the space became host to further screenings and encounters. In the years that followed, Ô Cách Ciné continued to reappear in other forms, extending beyond its original program and context.

Subsequent Iterations

Following its initial run in 2023, Ô Cách Ciné continued to reappear through projects developed by other collaborators and curators.
